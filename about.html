Our Story

BrightWave Digital LLC is a Pennsylvania-based digital solutions company specializing in digital marketing, social media management, and virtual assistant services for small and medium-sized businesses. We help brands grow their online presence, engage audiences, and streamline operations through tailored strategies and hands-on support. Whether you need help with content creation, ad campaigns, daily admin tasks, or full-service digital management — we’re your trusted partner for smart, efficient, and scalable support.

Founder & Digital Marketing Expert

CEO & Lead Strategist

Sarvekash Aggarwal is the founder of BrightWave Digital, a digital marketing agency dedicated to helping small and local businesses build a powerful online presence. With a passion for technology and business growth, Sarvekash brings a practical, results-driven approach to every project—whether it's designing responsive websites, optimizing for search engines, or setting up essential tools like Google Workspace. His mission is simple: make it easier for businesses to get discovered, earn trust online, and grow with confidence in the digital world.
