Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to see results from digital marketing?

Results vary depending on the service and your current situation. SEO typically takes 3-6 months to show significant results, while PPC campaigns can generate leads immediately. Social media and content marketing usually show engagement improvements within 1-2 months.

What makes BrightWave Digital different from other agencies?

We focus on data-driven strategies, transparent communication, and measurable results. Our team consists of certified experts who stay current with industry trends. We also provide dedicated account management and custom solutions tailored to your specific business goals.

Do you work with small businesses or just large corporations?

We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to enterprise companies. Our service packages are designed to accommodate different budgets and needs, ensuring every business can benefit from professional digital marketing.

What's included in your monthly reporting?

Our monthly reports include detailed analytics on website traffic, conversion rates, keyword rankings, social media engagement, ad performance, and ROI analysis. We also provide strategic recommendations for the following month.

Can you help with website development and design?

Yes! We offer complete web development services including custom design, responsive development, e-commerce solutions, and ongoing maintenance. All our websites are optimized for search engines and conversions.

What if I'm not satisfied with the results?

We're committed to your success. If you're not seeing the results we've outlined in your strategy, we'll work with you to adjust our approach. We also offer a satisfaction guarantee on our services with flexible contract terms.
